My Research


I am a research associate for the Cambridge arm of the MLSE study. We are currently recruiting patients with primary progressive aphasia to the study in Cambridge, London, and Manchester. We are also recruiting patients with other types of dementia, and patients with post-stroke aphasia. We aim to begin publishing results of the study in the coming months. I will update this page with my first-authored posters and papers relating to this project.
